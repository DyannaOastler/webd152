Our Story

Our Home

Pacific Trails Resort sits on the land that is considered home to Esselen Tribe for over 6,000 years. We are honored to call this place home and sharing the surrounding beauty from Big Sur to the ancient redwoods, and the vast Pacific Ocean.

Who We Are

The Resort is a family-owned retreat by the beautiful coast in Northern California who strongly believe disconnecting from everyday life will leave you refreshed whether if you're staying for a weekend or a week.

Our Values

The nature is the heart of everything we do. From the coastal trails to the ocean and the birds at sunrise, we believe that it is worth protecting and sharing. We are committed to preserving the beauty of the land that this resort calls home.
